About Me

I'm a 24-year-old Graphic Designer from Santa Rosa, Laguna, and a fresh graduate of Bachelor of Multimedia Arts from STI College Santa Rosa. I started learning Adobe Photoshop in elementary school through tutorials, and later developed skills in vector art, logos, and clothing designs using Adobe Illustrator.

I've designed posters for DJs, the Bigo community, and created clean, high-quality typography. I'm always looking to grow, take on new challenges, and work on creative projects with others.
